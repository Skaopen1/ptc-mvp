## 1. Training Center Mission

Pilot Training Center (PTC) at KPOC is an FAA-approved Part 141 pilot school committed to producing safe, competent, and proficient aviators. PTC delivers structured, stage-based flight training aligned with FAA minimum standards and industry best practices, from Private Pilot through Certified Flight Instructor certificates.

## 7. Enrollment Standards

1. Applicants must meet minimum age requirements for the certificate sought.
2. Valid FAA Medical Certificate required (as applicable per certificate level).
3. English language proficiency demonstrated.
4. Completed PTC enrollment application and initial interview.
5. Logbook documentation of prerequisites for advanced courses.

---

## 8. Completion and Graduation Standards

Each course requires:
- Satisfactory completion of all stage checks (no unsatisfactory items unresolved)
- Passing score (70% or higher) on the applicable FAA Airman Knowledge Test
- Satisfactory completion of PTC end-of-course written and oral test
- Chief Instructor endorsement on training record
- Submission of FAA Form 8710-1 or IACRA application

---

## 9. Recordkeeping Policy

Per 14 CFR §141.101, PTC retains the following for each student:
- Enrollment application and training agreement
- Ground and flight training records
- Stage check results (signed by evaluating instructor)
- Knowledge test results
- End-of-course test results
- All instructor endorsements

Minimum retention period: **3 years** from course completion or withdrawal.
