# PTC Part 141 Syllabus — Certified Flight Instructor (CFI) — ASEL
**Pilot Training Center | KPOC — Pocahontas Municipal Airport**  
**Regulatory Basis:** 14 CFR Part 141, Appendix F  
**Aircraft:** Cessna 172N  
**Document Version:** 1.0 | Effective Date: ___________

---

## 1. Course Objective

Upon completion of this course, the graduate will meet all aeronautical knowledge and experience requirements for the Certified Flight Instructor Certificate — Airplane Single-Engine Land (ASEL) as prescribed in 14 CFR Part 141, Appendix F, and will demonstrate the instructional knowledge, technique, and flight proficiency required to pass the FAA CFI Practical Test (PTS/ACS).

---

## 2. Regulatory Basis

| Requirement | Reference |
|-------------|-----------|
| Course approval | 14 CFR §141.55 |
| Training content | 14 CFR Part 141, Appendix F |
| Practical Test Standards | FAA-S-8081-6 (CFI PTS) |
| Knowledge Tests | FOI (FAA-CT-8080-6) + FIA (FAA-CT-8080-7) |
| Fundamentals of Instruction | 14 CFR §61.185 |

---

## 3. Entry Requirements

- Hold a Commercial Pilot Certificate (ASEL) or ATP
- Hold an Instrument Rating (ASEL)
- Minimum 250 hours total flight time (recommended; 14 CFR §61.183 requirements apply)
- Valid FAA Medical Certificate (3rd Class minimum)
- English language proficiency
- Completed PTC enrollment application and logbook review

---

## 5. Stage Structure

### Stage 1 — Fundamentals of Instruction
**Objective:** Student develops a thorough understanding of learning theory, teaching methods, lesson planning, and human behavior as applied to flight instruction.

#### Ground Lessons (Stage 1)

| Lesson | Topic | Hours |
|--------|-------|-------|
| G1-1 | Learning process — how people learn, memory, transfer | 2.0 |
| G1-2 | Human behavior — motivation, defense mechanisms | 1.5 |
| G1-3 | Effective communication — elements, barriers | 1.5 |
| G1-4 | Teaching process — objectives, standards | 1.5 |
| G1-5 | Lesson planning — content, sequence, time | 2.0 |
| G1-6 | Assessment and critique techniques | 1.5 |
| G1-7 | Instructional methods — lecture, demonstration-performance, guided discussion | 2.0 |
| G1-8 | Instructor responsibilities and professionalism | 1.5 |
| G1-9 | Human factors in instruction — stress, fatigue, IMSAFE | 1.5 |
| G1-10 | Risk management in instruction | 1.5 |
| G1-11 | FOI practice oral — mock examination | 2.0 |
| **Stage 1 Ground Total** | | **18.5 hrs** |

#### Flight Lessons (Stage 1)

| Lesson | Topic | Dual Hrs |
|--------|-------|----------|
| F1-1 | Instructional techniques — explanation from left seat | 1.0 |
| F1-2 | Preflight instruction demonstration | 1.0 |
| F1-3 | Flight control fundamentals — teaching primary effects | 1.0 |
| F1-4 | Slow flight and stalls — how to teach and demonstrate | 1.5 |
| F1-5 | Teaching traffic pattern and landings | 1.5 |
| **Stage 1 Flight Total** | **6.0 hrs** |

**Stage 1 Completion Standards:**
- Demonstrate knowledge of FOI topics at commercial-level oral proficiency
- Explain maneuvers clearly and accurately to ACS commercial tolerances
- Maintain aircraft control while providing instruction

---

### Stage 1 Check
**Evaluator:** Chief Instructor  
**Format:** Oral (45 min covering FOI topics) + dual flight (1.0 hr)  
**Areas:** All Fundamentals of Instruction topic areas; basic flight instruction technique  
**Standard:** Accurate, organized explanation; no critical errors in FOI material

---

### Stage 2 — Technical Subject Areas and Ground Instruction
**Objective:** Student develops mastery of all ground instruction subject areas required for CFI certification and can teach each to PPL/CPL students effectively.

#### Ground Lessons (Stage 2)

| Lesson | Topic | Hours |
|--------|-------|-------|
| G2-1 | Aerodynamics — teaching lift, drag, airfoil theory | 2.0 |
| G2-2 | Aircraft systems — teaching the C172N POH | 1.5 |
| G2-3 | Flight instruments — teaching pitot-static, gyroscopic | 1.5 |
| G2-4 | Regulations — teaching FAR Parts 61, 91 | 2.0 |
| G2-5 | Weather — teaching METAR, TAF, forecast products | 1.5 |
| G2-6 | Navigation — teaching charts, VOR, GPS | 1.5 |
| G2-7 | Weight & balance — teaching computations | 1.0 |
| G2-8 | Performance — teaching POH charts | 1.0 |
| G2-9 | Aeromedical factors — teaching I'M SAFE, hypoxia | 1.0 |
| G2-10 | Airport operations — teaching procedures | 1.0 |
| G2-11 | Teaching lesson plan construction | 2.0 |
| G2-12 | Technical subject oral practice | 2.0 |
| **Stage 2 Ground Total** | | **18.0 hrs** |

#### Flight Lessons (Stage 2)

| Lesson | Topic | Dual Hrs |
|--------|-------|----------|
| F2-1 | Teaching XC planning from left seat | 1.5 |
| F2-2 | Teaching VOR/GPS navigation | 1.5 |
| F2-3 | Teaching ground reference maneuvers | 1.5 |
| F2-4 | Teaching emergency procedures | 1.5 |
| F2-5 | Teaching short/soft field operations | 1.5 |
| F2-6 | Simulated student instruction — PPL stage | 1.5 |
| **Stage 2 Flight Total** | **9.0 hrs** |

**Stage 2 Completion Standards:**
- Deliver ground lessons on all technical subject areas without critical errors
- Demonstrate proper use of lesson plan structure
- Teach flight maneuvers with accurate explanations and appropriate correction techniques

---

### Stage 2 Check
**Evaluator:** Chief Instructor  
**Format:** Oral technical subject presentation (60 min) + dual flight instruction demonstration (1.5 hr)  
**Areas:** 3 randomly selected technical subject areas + flight instruction technique  
**Standard:** Accurate content, appropriate teaching methodology, safe flight instruction

---

### Stage 3 — CFI Checkride Preparation
**Objective:** Student integrates all CFI instructional skills into a complete, checkride-ready performance, meeting FAA CFI PTS standards.

#### Ground Lessons (Stage 3)

| Lesson | Topic | Hours |
|--------|-------|-------|
| G3-1 | CFI PTS review — all areas of operation | 2.0 |
| G3-2 | Stall/spin awareness — teaching requirements | 1.0 |
| G3-3 | Scenario-based training design | 1.0 |
| G3-4 | Mock oral — full CFI oral examination | 2.5 |
| G3-5 | Flight maneuver explanations — final preparation | 1.5 |
| **Stage 3 Ground Total** | | **8.0 hrs** |

#### Flight Lessons (Stage 3)

| Lesson | Topic | Dual Hrs |
|--------|-------|----------|
| F3-1 | Teaching all commercial maneuvers from left seat | 2.0 |
| F3-2 | Mock CFI checkride — full PTS run-through | 2.5 |
| F3-3 | Deficiency corrections | 1.5 |
| **Stage 3 Flight Total** | **6.0 hrs** |

---

### Stage 3 Check (End-of-Course Test)
**Evaluator:** Chief Instructor  
**Format:** Written test (60 questions, 70%) + oral (90 min) + flight (3.0 hr)  
**Areas:** All CFI PTS areas of operation  
**Standard:** All items satisfactory; no critical errors in instruction or safety of flight

---

## 6. End-of-Course Test Requirements

| Component | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Written Test | 60 questions, minimum 70% |
| Oral Examination | All PTS areas of operation; FOI and technical subjects |
| Flight Test | Full PTS CFI demonstration, all items satisfactory |
| FAA Knowledge Tests | Passed FOI + FIA prior to Practical Test |
| Logbook Verification | CPL + IR confirmed; 25 hrs dual as pilot receiving instruction in this course |
