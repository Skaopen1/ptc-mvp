## 2. programs.html (Programs Overview)

**Page Header**
- Title: "Certification Programs"
- Subheading: "Choose your path. We'll guide you there."

**Program Listings**

### Private Pilot
- Duration: ~60-80 flight hours
- Cost: Starting at $[amount]
- Key topics: VFR operations, cross-country, decision-making
- Prerequisites: Basic English proficiency, medical certificate
- Button: "View Details" / "Enroll Now"

### Instrument Rating
- Duration: ~40-50 flight hours
- Cost: Starting at $[amount]
- Key topics: IFR procedures, navigation, emergency protocols
- Prerequisites: Private Pilot certificate
- Button: "View Details" / "Enroll Now"

### Commercial Pilot
- Duration: ~55-70 flight hours
- Cost: Starting at $[amount]
- Key topics: Complex operations, multi-engine systems, practical test prep
- Prerequisites: Private + Instrument ratings
- Button: "View Details" / "Enroll Now"

**Comparison Table**
- Columns: Program | Hours | Duration | Cost | Prerequisite
- Rows: Private, Instrument, Commercial
